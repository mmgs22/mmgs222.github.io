Rising Intellect. Lifelong Learner. Fulltime Nerd.

Malcolm D. McGregor was born in Lansing, MI to two Detroit natives. Since a child, he has been fascinated with reading, technology, and learning. His entire life he has been devoted to his academics and considers himself an individual that is motivated to learn. Personally, he is a big sports fan, following various teams from the NBA, college basketball/football, and the NFL. He is a huge fan of the Black Power Movement, Harlem Renaissance, Civil Rights era, and all things that make up Black History. Currently, Malcolm attends The University of Texas at Austin and is pursuing a degree in Management Information Systems in the Red McCombs School of Business. In addition, he is pursuing a Minor in Black Studies, and hopes to work in the technology field upon graduation. Malcolm's long-term goals include starting his own business, creating a charity foundation for underprivileged black youths, and growing a beautiful family.
